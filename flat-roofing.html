FAQ

Common Questions

How much does a new flat roof cost? expand_more

Costs vary by material and size. Typically, a high-quality EPDM or GRP system starts from £80-£100 per square meter. We provide detailed, itemized quotes after a free site survey so there are no surprises.

How long does installation take? expand_more

An average residential garage or extension (approx 20sqm) usually takes 1-2 days. Larger commercial projects will have a bespoke timeline provided. We work efficiently to minimize disruption.

What is your warranty? expand_more

We offer a standard 10-year insurance-backed guarantee on all workmanship. Our premium GRP and EPDM systems often come with manufacturer warranties extending up to 20 or 25 years.
