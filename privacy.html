Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 2023 - How we handle, protect, and process your data.

Apex Industrial Roofing ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal data. This document outlines our Terms of Use, Privacy Policy, and Cookie Policy. By accessing or using our services, you agree to be bound by these terms.

01

Terms of Service

Our industrial roofing services are provided subject to the following terms and conditions. We reserve the right to update these terms at any time without prior notice.

1.1 Service Agreement

All roofing projects are subject to a formal written quotation. Acceptance of a quotation constitutes a binding contract between Apex Industrial and the client.

  • Quotations are valid for 30 days from the date of issue.
  • Emergency repairs are billed at our standard emergency call-out rates.
  • Materials remain the property of Apex Industrial until paid for in full.

1.2 Liability

While we carry comprehensive public liability insurance, we cannot be held responsible for pre-existing structural defects that were not visible during our initial inspection.

02

Privacy Policy

We respect your right to privacy. This section explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information in compliance with GDPR regulations.

2.1 Data Collection

We collect data necessary to provide our services and communicate with you effectively. This may include:

  • Contact information (Name, Phone Number, Email Address).
  • Property details and location data for site assessments.
  • Payment information for processing transactions (handled securely).

2.2 Data Usage

Your data is used strictly for operational purposes, including project planning, invoicing, and warranty registration. We do not sell your data to third parties.

Cookie Policy

Our website uses cookies to enhance user experience and analyze traffic. By continuing to browse the site, you consent to our use of cookies.
